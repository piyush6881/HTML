Image In HTML

  1. src="images/example.jpg"
  • :Specifies the location of the image file.
  1. alt="A scenic view of mountains during sunset"
  • : Provides alternative text that describes the image.
  1. Importance of src and alt Attributes:
  • src: Without the src attribute, the browser will not know which image to display.
  • alt: Without the alt attribute, if the image fails to load, there will be no textual description to indicate what the image was supposed to convey.
  • alt attribute to understand the content of the image, which can help with SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
