Detailled List or Description List (dl)

Meaning:
A description list is used to create a list of terms and their descriptions. It is useful for defining terms and their meanings or for pairing items with descriptions.
Usage:
To list terms and their definitions.
To pair names with descriptions, such as in a glossary.
