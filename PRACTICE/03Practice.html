State Capital and Attractions

  1. Andhra Pradesh

    • Capital: Amaravati
    • Famous Attraction:Amaravati Stupa
    • Amarāvati Stupa is a ruined Buddhist stūpa at the village of Amaravathi, Palnadu district, Andhra Pradesh

  2. Arunachal Pradesh

    • Capital: Itanagar
    • Famous Attraction:Ita Fort
    • Ita Fort - A historical fort built in the 14th-15th century with an impressive stone structure.

  3. Assam

    • Capital: Dispur
    • Famous Attraction:Kamakhya Temple
    • Kamakhya Temple - A renowned Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Kamakhya.

  4. Bihar

    • Capital: Patna
    • Famous Attraction: Mahavir Mandir
    • Mahavir Mandir - A popular Hanuman temple known for its religious significance and large gatherings.

  5. Chhattisgarh

    • Capital: Raipur
    • Famous Attraction: Nandan Van Zoo & Safari
    • Nandan Van Zoo & Safari - A large zoo and safari park home to diverse wildlife and lush greenery.

  6. Goa

    • Capital: Panaji
    • Famous Attraction: Basilica of Bom Jesus
    • Basilica of Bom Jesus - A UNESCO World Heritage Site containing the tomb of St. Francis Xavier.

  7. Gujarat

    • Capital: Gandhinagar
    • Famous Attraction: Akshardham Temple
    • Akshardham Temple - A magnificent temple complex dedicated to Lord Swaminarayan.

  8. Haryana

    • Capital: Chandigarh
    • Famous Attraction: Rock Garden
    • Rock Garden - A unique garden featuring sculptures made from industrial & home waste.

  9. Himachal Pradesh

    • Capital: Shimla
    • Famous Attraction: The Ridge
    • The Ridge - A spacious open area known for its scenic views and cultural activities.

  10. Jharkhand

    • Capital: Ranchi
    • Famous Attraction: Tagore Hill
    • Tagore Hill - A scenic hill named after Rabindranath Tagore, offering panoramic views.

  11. Karnataka

    • Capital: Bengaluru
    • Famous Attraction: Lalbagh Botanical Garden
    • Lalbagh Botanical Garden - A sprawling garden with diverse plant species and a glasshouse.
